Care Guide

Cleaning

Dry clean your suit only when necessary. Opt for spot cleaning and light brushing after each wear to remove surface dirt.

Storage

Hang your suit on a quality wooden hanger to maintain its shape. Allow the suit to rest for at least 24 hours between wears.

Maintenance

Steam rather than iron to remove wrinkles. Have your suit professionally pressed twice a season to maintain its elegance.

Travel

Use a proper garment bag when traveling. Upon arrival, hang your suit immediately to allow wrinkles to naturally release.
